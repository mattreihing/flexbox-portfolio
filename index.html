About Me

I graduated from the one and only Virginia Tech in 2016 with not one, but two liberal arts degrees. After working in marketing for a few years, I came across web development after talking with some friends about our careers.

From the start it’s felt like a natural fit. I love combining my creativity and analytical skills to create something totally new. Now I’m looking to learn as much as I can and build some great projects with similarly passionate people.

My competencies include:
